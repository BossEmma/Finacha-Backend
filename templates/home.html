About Us

We Help Our Clients To Grow Their Business

Finacha, an online banking platform where customers can rent Domiciliary cards for international transactions and currency management. These cards would be particularly useful for individuals who deal with multiple currencies and other online transactions or who travel frequently.

In today's fast-paced digital world, security and convenience are paramount when it comes to online payments. That's where Finacha Rent-a-Card comes in. Our innovative program offers a unique solution for those who want peace of mind when making purchases online..

Finacha's value proposition is to provide quality, convenient and affordable access to Domiciliary cards and day-to-day banking activities for students, young professionals and the general public.

At Finacha, we envision a future where online payments are not only secure but also seamlessly convenient for everyone. Our vision is to revolutionize the way people make purchases online by providing a trusted and innovative solution: Finacha Rent-a-Card
